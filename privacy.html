Introduction

The Work Works LLC ("we," "us," or "our") operates Bob's Bar (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

We are committed to protecting your privacy. We do not sell your personal data, and we do not display advertisements in our App.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

When you sign in using Apple Sign-In, we may receive:

  • Email Address: Your email address (or an Apple-generated relay email if you choose to hide your email)
  • Name: Your name, if you choose to share it during Apple Sign-In
  • User Identifier: A unique identifier provided by Apple for authentication purposes

Information You Create

When using the App, we store:

  • Favorites: Cocktail recipes you save to your favorites
  • Custom Variations: Your personalized recipe modifications and notes
  • Bar Inventory: Ingredients you mark as available in your bar
  • Preferences: App settings and preferences

Information Collected Automatically

We may collect certain information automatically, including:

  • Device type and operating system version
  • App usage analytics (features used, screens viewed)
  • Crash reports and error logs

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain the App's functionality
  • Sync your data across devices when signed in
  • Process and manage your subscription
  • Improve and optimize the App experience
  • Respond to your support requests
  • Send important updates about the App (with your consent)

We do NOT: Sell your personal data to third parties, display advertisements, or share your data for marketing purposes.

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using industry-standard practices:

  • Database: User data (favorites, variations, inventory) is stored in Supabase, a secure cloud database platform with encryption at rest and in transit
  • Authentication: We use Apple Sign-In, which provides secure, privacy-focused authentication
  • Encryption: All data transmitted between the App and our servers is encrypted using TLS/SSL

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

Apple Sign-In

We use Apple Sign-In for authentication. Apple's privacy practices are governed by Apple's Privacy Policy. Apple Sign-In provides privacy features like email relay and minimal data sharing.

RevenueCat

We use RevenueCat to manage subscriptions and in-app purchases. RevenueCat processes subscription data in accordance with their privacy policy. They receive only the information necessary to manage your subscription status.

Supabase

We use Supabase for secure data storage. Supabase provides encrypted database services and complies with industry security standards.

Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your data:

Access Your Data

You can view your favorites, variations, and inventory directly in the App.

Delete Your Data

You can request deletion of your account and all associated data by contacting us at brandon@theworkworks.net. We will process deletion requests within 30 days.

Data Portability

You can request a copy of your data by contacting our support team.

Opt-Out

You can use the App without signing in, though some features (sync, favorites, variations) require authentication.

Children's Privacy

Bob's Bar is rated 17+ due to alcohol-related content. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 17. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 17, please contact us immediately.

Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you services. If you request account deletion, we will delete your data within 30 days, except where we are required to retain certain information for legal or legitimate business purposes.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
