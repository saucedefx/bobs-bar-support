Introduction

The Work Works LLC ("we," "our," or "us") operates Bob's Bar mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your information when you use our App.

We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices. We do not sell your data, show advertisements, or track you across other apps or websites.

Information We Collect

Account Information

When you sign in to Bob's Bar using Apple Sign-In, we receive:

  • Your Apple ID email address (or private relay email)
  • Your name (if you choose to share it)
  • A unique user identifier from Apple

App Usage Data

To provide and improve our services, we store:

  • Favorites: Cocktails you mark as favorites
  • Variations: Custom recipe variations you create
  • MY BAR Inventory: Ingredients you add to your personal bar
  • User preferences: App settings and preferences

Subscription Information

Subscription data is managed through:

  • Apple App Store: Payment processing and subscription management
  • RevenueCat: Third-party service that helps us verify your subscription status

We do not store your payment information. All payments are processed securely by Apple.

Analytics and Diagnostics

We may collect anonymous usage analytics to improve the App, including:

  • App crashes and errors
  • Feature usage statistics
  • Device type and operating system version

This data is anonymized and cannot be used to identify you personally.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain the App's functionality
  • Sync your data across your devices
  • Verify your subscription status
  • Respond to your support requests
  • Improve the App's features and user experience
  • Prevent fraud and abuse
  • Comply with legal obligations

Data Storage and Security

Where We Store Your Data

Your data is stored securely using:

  • Supabase: Cloud database service for favorites, variations, and inventory
  • RevenueCat: Subscription management service

Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Secure authentication through Apple Sign-In
  • Row-level security policies on our database
  • Regular security audits and updates

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

Apple Sign-In

Authentication service provided by Apple Inc. Subject to Apple's Privacy Policy.

RevenueCat

Subscription management service. Subject to RevenueCat's Privacy Policy.

Supabase

Cloud database service. Subject to Supabase's Privacy Policy.

These services may collect and process data as described in their respective privacy policies. We carefully select third-party providers that meet our privacy and security standards.

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

We may disclose your information only in the following circumstances:

  • Service providers: To third-party services that help us operate the App (Supabase, RevenueCat)
  • Legal requirements: If required by law, court order, or government regulation
  • Protection of rights: To protect our rights, property, or safety, or that of our users
  • Business transfers: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets

Your Rights and Choices

Access and Control

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your favorites, variations, and inventory at any time
  • Request deletion of your account and all associated data

Account Deletion

To delete your account:

  1. Open Bob's Bar app
  2. Go to Settings ‚Üí Delete Account
  3. Confirm deletion

Or contact us at brandon@theworkworks.net to request account deletion.

Subscription Management

Manage or cancel your subscription through your Apple ID settings. Deleting the App does not automatically cancel your subscription.

Children's Privacy

Bob's Bar is rated 17+ and is not intended for users under the age of 17. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under the legal drinking age. If you believe we have collected information from a minor, please contact us immediately.

Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. When you delete your account:

  • Your personal data is deleted within 30 days
  • Anonymized analytics data may be retained for our records
  • Some data may be retained longer if required by law

International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your country of residence. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your data in accordance with this Privacy Policy and applicable laws.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes by:

  • Updating the "Last Updated" date
  • Posting a notice in the App
  • Sending an email (if you've provided one)

Your continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.

California Privacy Rights

If you are a California resident, you have specific rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA):

  • Right to know what personal information is collected
  • Right to know if personal information is sold or shared
  • Right to opt-out of sale of personal information (we do not sell data)
  • Right to delete personal information
  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising your rights

To exercise these rights, contact us at brandon@theworkworks.net.
