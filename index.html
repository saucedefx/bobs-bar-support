Frequently Asked Questions

What is Bob's Bar?
Bob's Bar is a premium cocktail discovery app for iOS featuring over 533 professional cocktail recipes. Use the MY BAR feature to see what you can make with ingredients you have, save your favorites, and create custom variations of recipes.
How does the MY BAR feature work?
Add ingredients from your home bar to your inventory, and MY BAR will show you all the cocktails you can make right now. It's the perfect way to discover drinks without needing to buy new ingredients.
What's included in the Premium subscription?
Premium subscribers get unlimited access to custom variations, enhanced MY BAR filtering, priority customer support, and early access to new features. Choose between monthly ($5.99) or annual ($41.99) subscriptions.
How do I manage my subscription?
Subscriptions are managed through your Apple ID. Go to Settings ‚Üí [Your Name] ‚Üí Subscriptions on your iPhone to view, change, or cancel your subscription. You can cancel at any time.
How do I sign in?
Bob's Bar uses Apple Sign-In for secure, privacy-focused authentication. Simply tap the "Sign in with Apple" button and authorize with Face ID, Touch ID, or your Apple ID password.
Can I create my own cocktail variations?
Yes! Premium subscribers can create unlimited custom variations of any recipe. Modify ingredients, adjust measurements, add your own notes, and save them to your personal collection.
Is my data private?
Absolutely. We take privacy seriously. Your favorites, variations, and inventory are stored securely and never shared with third parties. We don't sell your data, show ads, or track you. See our Privacy Policy for full details.
What if I have a problem or suggestion?
We'd love to hear from you! Contact us at brandon@theworkworks.net for support, bug reports, or feature suggestions. We typically respond within 24-48 hours.
What's the age requirement?
Bob's Bar is rated 17+ due to alcohol-related content. You must be of legal drinking age in your jurisdiction to use this app. Please drink responsibly.
Can I get a refund?
Refunds are handled through Apple's App Store. To request a refund, visit reportaproblem.apple.com and sign in with your Apple ID. Note that refund policies are determined by Apple.
