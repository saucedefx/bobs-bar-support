About Bob's Bar

Bob's Bar is your ultimate cocktail companion, featuring over 533 professionally curated recipes. Whether you're a home bartender or a cocktail enthusiast, Bob's Bar helps you discover, create, and perfect your favorite drinks.

Key Features

  • 533+ Cocktail Recipes - Classic and modern cocktails with detailed instructions
  • My Bar - Track your ingredients and discover what you can make
  • Favorites - Save your go-to recipes for quick access
  • Custom Variations - Create and save your own recipe tweaks
  • Batch Calculator - Scale recipes for parties and events
  • Inventory Tracking - Keep track of your bar supplies

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I cancel my subscription?

You can cancel your subscription at any time through your iPhone's Settings app. Go to Settings > [Your Name] > Subscriptions > Bob's Bar > Cancel Subscription. Your premium access will continue until the end of your current billing period.

How do I restore my purchases on a new device?

Open Bob's Bar, go to the subscription screen, and tap "Restore Purchases." Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID you used to purchase the subscription.

How do I delete my account?

To delete your account and all associated data, please contact us at brandon@theworkworks.net with the subject line "Account Deletion Request." We will process your request within 30 days.

Why do I need to sign in?

Signing in with Apple allows us to sync your favorites, custom variations, and bar inventory across devices. We use Apple Sign-In for your privacy and security.

What's included in Premium?

Premium subscribers get unlimited access to all features including unlimited drink saves, advanced "My Bar" filtering, favorites and collections, custom notes and ratings, and the batch calculator.
